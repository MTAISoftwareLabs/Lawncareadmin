Phase 1: Customer Website & User Experience
1. Onboarding & Account Management

User Registration & Login: Seamless sign-up and login using email, phone, or social media accounts.

Profile Management: Users can manage their personal information, saved shipping addresses, and communication preferences.

Guest Checkout: A quick, frictionless purchase path that does not require account creation.

Order History & Tracking: Users have a dedicated section to view all past orders, check real-time status, and download invoices.

2. Product Discovery & Browsing

Personalized Homepage: A dynamic homepage showcasing promotional banners, featured products, "Deals of the Day," and flash sales.

Advanced Product Search: A smart search bar with autocomplete suggestions and robust filtering options (by category, price, brand, ratings, etc.) and sorting capabilities.

Category Browsing: Intuitive navigation through a well-structured hierarchy of product categories and subcategories.

Engagement Sections: Dedicated areas for "Recently Viewed Items," "Popular Products," and a blog for content marketing and SEO.

3. Product Evaluation & Decision

Detailed Product Pages: Comprehensive pages with high-quality image galleries, detailed descriptions, specifications, and customer ratings and reviews.

Wishlist Management: Allows users to save favorite products for later.

Shopping Cart: Users can add products, review their selections, and modify quantities before proceeding to checkout.

4. Checkout & Payment (Razorpay Only)

Secure Checkout Process: A streamlined, multi-step process to review cart items, select a shipping address, and choose a delivery option.

Razorpay Integration: A secure, seamless payment experience powered by Razorpay, supporting all its available methods (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets, etc.) in a single integration.

Promotions at Checkout: Users can apply discount coupons, promo codes, or gift cards to their purchase.

5. Post-Purchase & Support

Order Confirmation: Immediate confirmation via email/SMS after a successful purchase.

Real-Time Order Tracking: Users can track their order's journey with live status updates from confirmation to delivery.

Easy Returns & Refunds: A simple, transparent process for users to initiate returns and request refunds.

Multi-Channel Support: Users can get help through live chat, email, phone support, and a comprehensive help center/FAQ section.

Phase 2: Administrative Control & Backend Management
1. Centralized Oversight & Analytics

Admin Dashboard with Real-Time Analytics: A powerful command center showing key metrics like total sales, revenue, active users, new orders, and conversion rates.

Reports & Analytics: Generate deep-dive reports on sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and revenue analytics.

2. User & Catalog Management

User Management: View, monitor, and manage all customer accounts and their activity.

Product Catalog Management: Full control over the product inventory - add, edit, categorize, and manage product listings, images, descriptions, pricing, and stock levels.

Inventory Tracking & Stock Management: System-wide inventory management with low-stock alerts to ensure products are never oversold.

3. Order & Financial Operations

Order & Transaction Management: View and manage every order on the platform. Update statuses, handle cancellations, and monitor the entire order lifecycle.

Razorpay Settlement Monitoring: Monitor transactions, track payouts from Razorpay, and reconcile financial records.

Return, Refund, and Dispute Management: A central system to efficiently process all customer return and refund requests.

4. Platform Operations, Marketing & Security

Promotional Campaign Management: Create and manage site-wide promotions, discount codes, gift cards, and schedule flash sales or seasonal offers.

Banner & Ad Management: Control the promotional banners and hero images displayed on the homepage and other key pages.

CMS for Static Pages: Easily create and update content for pages like About Us, Contact, FAQs, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Service.

Notification Management: Configure and send automated notifications to users via Email, SMS, and Push for orders, promotions, and security alerts.

Fraud Detection & Security Monitoring: Implement rules and monitor for suspicious activity to prevent fraud and secure customer data.

Tax and Compliance Management: Configure tax settings (like GST) automatically during checkout and ensure platform compliance with regulations.

Phase 3: Cross-Functional & Platform-Wide Features
Personalized Product Recommendations: AI-driven suggestions on the homepage, product pages, and in emails to boost engagement and sales.

Multi-Language and Multi-Currency Support: To cater to a wider audience (if applicable).

Mobile Responsive Design: Ensures a flawless user experience on all devices - desktop, tablet, and smartphone.
